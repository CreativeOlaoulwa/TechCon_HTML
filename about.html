Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of developers and innovators. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most anticipated global tech conferences, attracting thousands of participants from around the world.

Our Mission

TechCon 2024 is committed to inspiring innovation, fostering community, and shaping the future of technology. Our goal is to empower creators and thought leaders to make meaningful impact across industries.

Notable Past Speakers

  • Maya Fields – Renowned AI researcher and author, Maya shared groundbreaking insights into ethical AI design at TechCon 2022.

  • David Lee – As a leading cybersecurity consultant, David has helped global organizations secure their digital assets. He spoke on zero-trust architecture in 2023.
